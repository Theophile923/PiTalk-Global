Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

PiTalk Global is built to remove language barriers for Pi Network pioneers while keeping conversations as private as possible. This page explains what data the app handles and why.

Audio and conversations

  • Voice audio is processed only to produce a translation and is not stored long-term by default.
  • You may opt in to save a conversation's text transcript so you can refer back to it later. Nothing is saved without this choice.
  • You can set how long any saved history is kept, and delete it at any time.

Language preferences

The language you speak and the language you want to hear are saved on your device so you don't have to re-select them every time. This is stored locally in your browser and is not shared with other pioneers.

Pi Authentication and payments

  • Signing in uses the official Pi SDK. PiTalk Global receives your Pi username and the permissions you approve — nothing more.
  • Subscription payments are processed through the Pi payment flow. PiTalk Global does not handle or store your wallet credentials.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data to advertisers or third parties.
  • We don't record calls without your explicit opt-in.
  • We don't share your conversations with other users or the public.

Your choices

You can clear your saved language preferences and any local history at any time by clearing this site's data in your browser settings, or from within the app once history controls are available.
